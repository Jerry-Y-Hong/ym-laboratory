## 1. Purpose

The purpose of this memo is to record potential future architectural concepts discussed during the Phase 01–40 architectural review. These concepts represent exploratory ideas that may become relevant after the YM-LAB AI Enterprise Platform enters real-world production operation.

This document serves strictly as an informal design reference. It carries no governance authority, is not part of the active roadmap, and does not alter any frozen baseline or project structure.

---

## 2. Current Decision & Official Project Scope

### Official Project Scope
- **Phases 01 through 40** constitute the complete, unified **Core Foundation** of the YM-LAB Enterprise Ecosystem.
- **Phases 01 through 40** remain the exclusive official implementation and release target for **YM-LAB AI Enterprise Platform v1.0**.
- **No additional architectural expansion** is included in the current implementation scope or active baseline roadmap.

---

## 3. Future Evolution Strategy (Exploratory Concepts)

The following concepts represent prospective ideas that may be explored following successful platform operation, if and when justified by actual ecosystem growth and operational evidence:

- **Collaboration Governance**: Frameworks for multi-tenant and multi-organization AI collaboration.
- **Knowledge Governance**: Advanced curation and sovereignty mechanisms for federated domain knowledge.
- **Research Collaboration**: Open and secure integration pipelines for academic and industrial research partners.
- **Partner Management**: B2B onboarding, API quota governance, and partner SLA management.
- **Product Collaboration**: Co-development workflows for derivative Q-Code AI products.
- **Certification Framework**: Compliance and quality certification standards for third-party AI agents and integrations.
- **Ecosystem Architecture**: Macro-level architectural patterns for multi-enterprise AI network federations.

> [!NOTE]
> The concepts listed above are illustrative examples only. They do **NOT** constitute approved roadmap items, active requirements, or scheduled deliverables.

---

## 4. Guiding Principle

All future architectural considerations are governed by the following core philosophy:

> **Build the Platform First.**
>
> **Grow the Ecosystem Next.**
>
> **Standardize from Real Experience.**

## 5. Future Review Policy

- **Phases 41 through 50** are reserved as an **Evolution Phase** tier for potential post-v1.0 concepts.
- The specific scope, priorities, and requirements for any future phase within this tier shall be determined strictly by actual operational experience and empirical evidence.
- Future architectural development must remain **evidence-driven rather than assumption-driven**.

### Revision Triggers

Future architectural review may be initiated when one or more of the following conditions are observed:

- Multiple external organizations actively participate in the platform.
- Stable collaboration workflows become established.
- Governance requirements emerge from production operation.
- Ecosystem expansion requires standardized architectural frameworks.
