# 1. Executive Summary

Phase 26 establishes the **AI Autonomous Strategic Intelligence System (ASIS)** as the Strategic Brain of the YM-LAB Autonomous Enterprise Architecture.

ASIS extends the decision and execution capabilities established in Phase 25 (AADS / AEDES) by adding strategic reasoning, long-term trend & demand prediction, multi-scenario digital twin simulation, multi-objective Pareto optimization, persistent strategic memory, and closed-loop feedback learning.

ASIS fully complies with **ADF v3.1** and maintains 100% backward compatibility with all prior phases (Phases 01–25) without modifying any established deliverables or governance standards.
