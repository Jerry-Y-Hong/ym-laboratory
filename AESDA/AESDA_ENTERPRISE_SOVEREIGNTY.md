## 2. Core Sovereignty Principles

- **Data Residency Enforcement**: Sensitive Q-Code ontologies and user datasets are bound strictly to authorized geographical and legal jurisdictions.
- **Cryptographic Independence**: Cryptographic keys are governed independently of underlying cloud infrastructure providers.
- **Regulatory Mapping**: Continuous compliance mapping against international data protection regulations and applicable security frameworks.
