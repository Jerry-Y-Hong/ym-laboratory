## 2. Mission Assurance Controls

- **Critical Path Isolation**: Guarantees dedicated compute and priority bandwidth for Tier 0 Mission Critical services.
- **Service Assurance SLAs**: Establishes 99.999% availability targets for governance and knowledge retrieval engines.
- **Continuous Mission Auditing**: Real-time telemetry audits critical path health and alerts human operators instantly upon SLA degradation.
