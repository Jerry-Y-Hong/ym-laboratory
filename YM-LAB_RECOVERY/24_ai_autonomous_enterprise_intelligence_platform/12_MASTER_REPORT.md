# 1. Executive Summary

Phase 24 establishes the **AI Autonomous Enterprise Intelligence Platform (AEIP)** as the intelligence and decision layer of the YM-LAB Autonomous Enterprise Architecture.

AEIP integrates all data produced by Phases 01–23, constructs a unified Enterprise Knowledge Model, and delivers real-time enterprise intelligence, multi-horizon predictions, decision recommendations, executive support, and full governance coverage.

AEIP acts as the **Enterprise Brain** of YM-LAB, transforming operational data into actionable intelligence for every level of the organization.
