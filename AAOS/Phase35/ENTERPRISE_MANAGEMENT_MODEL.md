# ENTERPRISE_MANAGEMENT_MODEL.md

## Overview
The Enterprise Management Model defines how organizational units, processes, KPIs, and data flows are orchestrated within the **AI Enterprise Management System (AEMS)**.

### Core Concepts
- **Organizational Units** – Business domains, AI teams, and support functions.
- **Process Layers** – Planning, Execution, Monitoring, and Governance.
- **Data Flow** – From raw operational data → analytic pipelines → dashboards & decisions.
- **KPI Mapping** – Each unit links to measurable KPIs defined in `PERFORMANCE_MANAGEMENT.md`.
