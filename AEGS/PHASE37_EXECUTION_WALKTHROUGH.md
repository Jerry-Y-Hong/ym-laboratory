In accordance with the **Project Owner Final Recommendations**, the **Phase 37: AI Enterprise Governance System (AEGS)** architecture deliverables have been meticulously refined to achieve absolute alignment with **ADF v3.1 Governance Standards** and the YM-LAB Enterprise Architecture.

All deliverable documents enforce technology neutrality, vendor independence, and architecture-level abstraction. Specifically:
1. All instances of authority terminology use **Human Governance Authority**, **Human Decision Authority**, or **Project Owner Authority**.
2. Algorithm- and hash-specific expressions use **Cryptographic Integrity Verification**, **Cryptographic Integrity Binding**, and **Enterprise Integrity Verification**.
3. Schema and format terms use **Standardized Metadata Definition**, **Standardized Policy Metadata Definition**, and **Enterprise Metadata Definition**.

5. Process and change terminology uses **Governance Change Management Process**, **Enterprise Change Review Workflow**, and **Procedure** (avoiding protocol/implementation-level terms).
